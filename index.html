About the game

Bestagon Defence is a non-repetitive tower defence. Often tower defences are repetitive, you find a strategy, and it all becomes rather similar. So we've taken rogue-genre elements to spice it up, all upgrades can be applied to multiple turrets, and the shop is randomised every time you open it! Sounds fun? Check out the game on itch.io!

How to play

Bestagon Defence is a tower defence game! This means you've got to place turrets to stop enemies reaching the end goal for as long as possible.

The Goal

You begin with each level with a certain amount of , and each time an enemy reaches they end, you lose at least one. You can gain more through the shop. If you lose them all, it's game over. The aim is therefore to make is as far as possible before you lose, aiming to top the leaderboards!

The Shop

The shop is here to help! You can use the hard earned [gold] you gain from killing enemies by opening the shop. The price increases each time however. Each time you open the shop you'll get some choices. The main three are either turrets or modules (turret upgrades). You can only pick one thing before the shop closes. These three are randomised each time the shop opens.
